Who is "Made of Funk"?

We are street dance members from Made of Funk originated in Tainan. We started our journey in 2020, and there has been countless amount of adventures we went through. The majority of our appearances can be found in campus, at street dance competition, and pretty much everywhere fun. We provide lectures to students who want to learn street dance, as well as competing at competitions to spread our funkiness. Most importantly, we try to develop a lifestyle and keep being inspired and inspiring!
